Quick answers

  • How do I add an idea? On the home screen, type a few words in the field at the top and press return. You can also capture from the Home Screen widget, the Share sheet, Control Center, or by telling Siri “Add to Figmint.”
  • It isn’t suggesting anything yet. Figmint draws an idea out a little at a time — open an idea and answer the question it asks (a single word is fine). After a few notes, it proposes one small first step.
  • My ideas aren’t syncing across devices. Sync uses your own private iCloud. Make sure both devices are signed in to the same Apple ID and that iCloud Drive is on for Figmint (iOS Settings → your name → iCloud).
  • Is my data private? Yes — everything runs and stays on your device, and your ideas sync only to your own private iCloud. See the Privacy Policy.

About

Figmint keeps everything on your device, runs its AI on-device, and is subscription-free. No account, no tracking, no ads.
